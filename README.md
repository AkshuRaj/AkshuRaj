# 💫 About Me:
Hey there! I’m Akshayaa <br><br>I'm a software systems student with a chaotic kind of curiosity that pulls me into anything interesting until i figure it out. I lean toward backend, but i don’t stay in one lane for long; I like designing APIs, experimenting with AI/ML, exploring devops, and occasionally turning all of that into visually cool interfaces. I approach code a little like design — clean, structured, but with personality — because i care about how things work *and* how they feel. I’m a night owl, a creative at heart, and someone who enjoys building, breaking, and rebuilding ideas until they finally click (which usually feels like champagne and confetti in my head). I’m the kind of person who follows curiosity wherever it goes and stays long enough to make sense of it.<br>
